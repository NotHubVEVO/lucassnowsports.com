Did you know how great blind snowboarders can be?

This might be hard to believe , but blind snowboards are actually very talented!
They can use their sense of hearing and smell to help guide them down the slopes.
While other snowboarders need to worry about purchasing goggles to keep snow
out of their eyes, the blind boarders can just get on without goggles!
As a blind person myself , I go snowboarding with my service dog down the slopes.
We are crazy!
